Welcome to Couch Potatoes, an explosive game where you can sabotage your friends, throw them off cliffs, freeze them in their tracks,
and much more in a game of hot potato designed to create maximal chaos.
You can play with a variety of different characters on your epic journey to blow up your friendships!
Please join our discord server through the link in our bio. The game is still early in development,
but we're pushing for a playtesting period in the near future, so sign up if you're interested!
