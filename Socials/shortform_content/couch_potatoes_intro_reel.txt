Welcome to Couch Potatoes, an explosive game where you can sabotage your friends,
throw them off cliffs, freeze them in their tracks,
and much more in an exciting game of hot potato designed to create maximal chaos.
The game will feature lots of unique power-ups, such as the shockwave and freezing
you just saw, and it'll have a variety of different characters to play as
on your epic journey to blow up your friendships!
This game is still early in development,
but we're pushing for a playtesting period in the near future,
so sign up if you're interested!
The link's in our bio, and while you're at it,
